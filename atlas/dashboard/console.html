Desk Scorecard — Graded Against SPY

Every committee memo tracked against what the stock actually did next, SPY-relative (ADR-0009: the market is the fund's honest alternative). BUY vindicated when it beat SPY at the horizon; REJECT vindicated when the desk dodged an underperformer — even one that rose, but rose less. HOLD and shadow memos are tracked, never rated. Every rate is shown against the DART — what a direction-blind throw at the same tracked outcomes would score — because the base rate is free: an always-REJECT desk in a falling market matches the dart exactly, and its edge reads zero. The DISSENT is graded too, as the exact complement of the call for directional memos.

Learning Loop — Measured, Never Applied

Every matured scorecard outcome is labeled into the learning plane the night it matures: the call graded by the one scorecard rule, each specialist seat graded against the realized excess sign, red flags validated when the flagged risk materialized.
