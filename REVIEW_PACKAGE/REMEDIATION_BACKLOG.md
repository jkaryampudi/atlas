## Severity legend
**High** — could materially mislead an investment/risk decision, or a real safety/security control is
absent. **Medium** — misleads an engineering/ops/governance judgment, or a documented control is
inactive. **Low** — hygiene, doc drift, or an unreproduced-but-not-refuted claim.
