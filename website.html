Ten articles. These six run at the boundary.

I
SEPARATION OF PLANES

III
SCHEMA OR SILENCE
Malformed data never reaches a decision. It dies at the boundary, loudly.

IV

The risk engine vetoes before merit is argued. Conviction is not a position size.
